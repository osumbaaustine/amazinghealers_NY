Home Services

Here's what we are good at.

Why Amazing Healers?

We provide health care services in home settings as well as group home residential settings. All our care is individualized based on the need of the client and their family. Each case is unique and is treated as such. Our residential services comes in a home-like group home setting with a maximum of 6 residents per home. Our goal is to provide a home-like setting where each resident feels loved, supported, served and inspired to be the best possible version of themselves.

Nursing Services

We provide home health nursing services provided by Licensed Practical Nurse (LPN), Registered Nurse (RN).

Personal Support

We provide assistance and training to the recipient in activities of daily living, such as eating, bathing, dressing, personal hygiene, and preparation of medicines.

Respite Care

We provide relief to the caregiver by assisting in maintaining a recipients own home and property as a clean, sanitary, and safe environment.

Residential Services

We provide residential services in a home-like group home setting with a maximum of 6 residents per home
