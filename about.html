About Us

Our Story Our Mission Statement Our Vision

We are an approved Medicaid provider;

Amazing Healers Health care services was born out of real-life experiences of a Nurse Practitioner with almost 20 years of nursing experience who is also a mother to 3 children with special needs. Having cared for a wide variety of patients in home settings for 5 years, we fully understand the importance of home health.

When you tell us you need respite care, or home health nurse or you need to find residence in a group home for your loved one, we get it because we have lived it!

To create a family-oriented and compassionate environment for each person entrusted to our care

To inspire and help them to become the best possible version of themselves physically, emotionally, and spiritually.

To demonstrate that disability is not an inability

and to become the preferred care provider for all persons with disability in the communities we serve.

We commit to enhancing the quality of life of each person entrusted to our care through high-quality exceptional and personalized care provided in an atmosphere of compassion, hospitality, and respect for the dignity of each person.

Our Core Values

Integrity

We hold ourselves accountable to the highest ethical standards, demonstrating sincerity, professionalism, and authenticity.

Service to Humanity

Service is the essence of our culture. We serve those in need with meekness, charm, and gratitude.

Compassion

We seek to create a warm and compassionate environment by showing genuine kindness and empathy and offering hope and encouragement to every resident and employee.

Respect

We believe each of our residents and employees are unique and deserve to be treated with dignity and courtesy. We believe that respect for others is the foundation of honor and the basis of integrity.
